About Us

Dr. Mashamba



Dr Mashamba is a former senior lecturer in Business Management in institutions of Higher Education and long time commercial manager in various organizations of the business sector in Tanzania.

At Mashamba's Store, we are driven by a passion for education and a commitment to providing learners with high-quality academic resources. Our mission is to empower students to excel in their studies and careers through a curated selection of books and engaging online lectures. Whether you're looking to enhance your business knowledge or pursue a new career, we have the right resources for you.

We specialize in offering materials and expert-led tuition in essential areas of Business Management, including:

  • Business Finance
  • Costing and Management Accounting
  • Principles of Financial Accounting
  • Marketing Principles and Practice
  • Supply Chain Management
  • Quantitative Techniques in Management Decisions

Our vision is to become the leading online bookstore and tuition provider, delivering valuable education at affordable prices. We invite you to join the Mashamba community and take the next step in your academic journey.

Thank you for choosing Mashamba Store and Tuition Center, your partner in learning.
